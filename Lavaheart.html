We chose to take this game because we were ashamed of the quality of the official website of Geometry Dash. as we both love this game we ere very commited and put a lot of passion into it. This was a project that is precious to me as we had a really nice synergy

we had a hard time doing the carousel because the text desribing the image didn't want to change. When we resolved the issue we felt great releaf and an immence feeling of accomplishment, that is why this was the core of our project.

This is the final render of the carousel with the description of the rules,how to buy the game and its price. I am very happy with the final product
